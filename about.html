My journey began in 2021 as an eCommerce Manager at a cycle company, where I learned how online businesses truly function—from customer behavior to conversion psychology. While working full-time, I taught myself website development and quickly realized one thing: a well-built website directly impacts sales and brand growth.

The real turning point came at BXB Social. I built high-converting websites for brands like BurgerBae and Urban Needs, while also mastering Meta Ads strategy. By combining performance-driven UI with data-backed advertising, I helped brands scale to ₹35–40 lakhs in monthly sales. This phase defined my approach: design and marketing must work together to deliver real results.

Today, I'm working with Confidence Clothing, where I built their website from the ground up, manage their online sales, and am currently developing a custom ERP system to streamline operations and support scale. Alongside this, I launched my own dropshipping brand Decor Bae, generating ₹2 lakhs in just 2 months from zero, and built Balance Is the New Green, a mental-health focused platform for expert Janvi Sikaria. With 10+ successful websites and proven growth outcomes, I now build performance-driven digital systems that don't just look good—they convert, scale, and sell.
