My Story

What started as a curiosity-driven exploration into web development in 2020 quickly turned into a mission. While many developers focus solely on aesthetics or others purely on marketing metrics, I saw an opportunity to bridge both worlds—creating websites that don't just look stunning but actually convert visitors into customers.

My breakthrough came with Decor Bae, where I combined high-end web design with data-driven advertising strategies to generate over ₹22 lakhs in sales. That success validated my approach: technology and marketing aren't separate—they're two sides of the same coin. Since then, I've built 50+ websites and managed campaigns that consistently deliver 300%+ ROI.

Today, I integrate AI and automation into everything I build, ensuring clients get not just a website or ad campaign, but a complete growth system that works 24/7. What makes me different? I don't just execute—I strategize, optimize, and continuously improve results based on real data.
